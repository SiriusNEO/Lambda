Chaofan Lin (in Chinese: 林超凡)

I'm an undergraduate at ACM Honors Class, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Yong Yu. Now I'm very fortunate to work with Yuqing Yang in the System Group of Microsoft Research Asia. Previously I'm a research intern at Apache TVM community, working closely with Tianqi Chen and focusing on the design of relax (a graph-level IR for tvm) training workflow.

My research interest mainly lies in computer systems and architecture. I enjoy crafting systems and/or exploring efficient algorithms to bridge the gap between efficiency (low-level) and usability (user-side).

I believe there is no silver bullet in designing and architecting. Hence there is no perfect solution for all ad-hoc reallife problems. However, I will try my best to narrow the gap between theory and practice, hoping that one day everyone can appreciate and benefit from those great ideas in Computer Science.
